Community Guidelines

Creating a respectful debate environment

Our Vision

Arment is a platform for constructive debate where ideas clash, not people. We believe in the power of respectful discourse to sharpen thinking, challenge assumptions, and find truth through rigorous argument.

Core Principles

1. Engage in Good Faith

Participate in debates with honest intent. Present your genuine beliefs, respond to opponents' actual arguments, and acknowledge valid points.

2. Focus on Ideas, Not People

Attack arguments, not the people making them. Personal insults, character attacks, and ad hominem fallacies have no place in debate.

3. Support Your Claims

Back up your arguments with evidence, logic, and reasoning. Unsupported assertions weaken your position.

4. Listen and Respond

Actually engage with your opponent's arguments. Don't just wait for your turn to talk – respond to what they've said.

5. Admit When You're Wrong

Changing your mind in light of new evidence is a strength, not a weakness. Intellectual honesty builds credibility.

What We Encourage

  • ✅ Well-reasoned arguments
  • ✅ Evidence-based claims
  • ✅ Respectful disagreement
  • ✅ Acknowledging good points
  • ✅ Asking clarifying questions
  • ✅ Staying on topic
  • ✅ Using proper debate techniques
  • ✅ Fact-checking yourself

What's Not Allowed

Zero Tolerance Violations

These will result in immediate account suspension:

  • ❌ Harassment, bullying, or threats
  • ❌ Hate speech or discrimination
  • ❌ Doxxing or sharing private information
  • ❌ Sexual content or solicitation
  • ❌ Illegal activity or advocacy
  • ❌ Impersonation

Prohibited Debate Tactics

  • ❌ Personal attacks
  • ❌ Strawman arguments
  • ❌ Deliberate misinformation
  • ❌ Spam or repetitive posts
  • ❌ Off-topic arguments
  • ❌ Trolling or bad faith participation
  • ❌ Vote manipulation

Content Guidelines

Debate Topics

You can debate almost anything, but topics must not:

  • Promote violence or illegal activity
  • Target protected groups with hate
  • Share private information
  • Violate intellectual property rights

Arguments & Posts

  • Keep arguments relevant to the debate topic
  • Use clear, understandable language
  • Cite sources when making factual claims
  • Don't plagiarize others' work

Reporting & Moderation

How to Report

See content that violates these guidelines? Use the report button (exclamation mark icon) and select a reason. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

What Happens Next

When content is reported:

  1. Our team reviews the report and context
  2. If it violates guidelines, we take action
  3. You'll receive a notification about the outcome
  4. Repeat offenders face escalating consequences

Enforcement Actions

  • Warning: First-time minor violations
  • Content Removal: Violating posts are deleted
  • Temporary Suspension: 7-30 days for repeated violations
  • Permanent Ban: Zero tolerance violations or repeated offenses

Appeals

If you believe a moderation decision was made in error, you can appeal by contacting [email protected] with your account details and explanation.

Debate Etiquette

Before You Debate

  • Research your position
  • Understand the opposing view
  • Prepare your main arguments
  • Gather evidence and sources

During the Debate

  • Stay calm and respectful
  • Listen to your opponent
  • Address their actual points
  • Don't strawman their position
  • Concede valid points
  • Ask for clarification when needed

After the Debate

  • Accept the community's verdict gracefully
  • Learn from your mistakes
  • Thank your opponent
  • Reflect on what you learned

Building a Better Community

Every member shapes Arment's culture. By following these guidelines, you help create an environment where:

  • Ideas are tested through rigorous debate
  • Logic and evidence trump emotion
  • People learn and grow from disagreement
  • Respectful discourse is the norm
  • Truth emerges through honest inquiry
