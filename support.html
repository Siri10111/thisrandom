Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a debate?

Browse the Debates tab and tap on any debate marked as "Open". Choose your position (For or Against) and tap "Join Debate" to enter.

How is the winner determined?

After arguments are presented, the community votes on which side presented the most compelling case. The debater with the most votes wins.

What are debate points?

Points are earned by winning debates, posting quality arguments, and receiving upvotes from the community. Points determine your position on the leaderboard.

Can I debate any topic?

You can create debates on almost any topic, but content must follow our Community Guidelines. Debates promoting hate, violence, or illegal activity are not allowed.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the report icon (exclamation mark) on any debate or argument. Select a reason and provide details. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Type "DELETE" to confirm. This permanently removes all your data.

Getting Started

  1. Create Your Account: Sign up with your email and choose a username.
  2. Browse Debates: Explore the Debates tab to see ongoing and open debates.
  3. Join or Create: Join an open debate or create your own topic.
  4. Present Arguments: Make your case with clear, logical arguments.
  5. Build Your Reputation: Win debates to earn points and climb the leaderboard.

Account Help

Reset Password

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link.

Change Email

Go to Profile → Settings → Account Settings to update your email address.

Update Profile

Tap your profile picture or go to Profile → Edit Profile to change your bio, avatar, and display name.

Delete Account

Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Safety & Moderation

Our Commitment

Arment is committed to maintaining a respectful debate environment. We have zero tolerance for:

  • Harassment or bullying
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Violence or threats
  • Spam or misinformation
  • Inappropriate content

Reporting Content

See something that violates our guidelines? Use the report button. All reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

Blocking Users

You can block any user by tapping their profile and selecting "Block User". Blocked users cannot interact with your content.
